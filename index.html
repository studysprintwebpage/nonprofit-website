Adhesive Bandages
Assorted sizes for minor cuts, scrapes, and blisters.
🧻

How It Works

From assembly to your hands

Care Compass works alongside partner organizations to get boxes into the hands of people who need them most.

01
We source & assemble
Kind donors provide money, and CareCompass leaders work to purchase and coordinate materials, money, and outreach. Voulenteers then pack first aid supplies and OTC medications into care boxes that they assemble themselves.
02
We partner with orgs
Boxes are distributed through trusted community organizations already serving people in need.
03
Boxes reach people
Recipients receive their box with all supplies plus our health literacy pamphlet — free of charge.
04
Knowledge transfers
The included pamphlet empowers recipients to use supplies safely and understand basic medical care.
"

Access to clear health information shouldn't be a barrier.

Our Mission

Why we do this

We started Care Compass because we saw a gap: many people lacked not just basic medical supplies, but the knowledge to use them safely. Our boxes solve both problems.

  • ✓ Access without barriers. Every box is completely free to the recipient — always.
  • ✓ Education alongside resources. Supplies are only half the solution. Our pamphlet builds real health confidence.
  • ✓ Community-powered distribution. We work through existing organizations to reach people where they already are.
  • ✓ Dignity at the core. Care is delivered without judgment, applications, or conditions.
